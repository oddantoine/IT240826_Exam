Do you know you can access your colour settings fairly easily?
The first thing to do is to look at the Windows settings by opening the start menu (or pressing the Windows key on your keyboard) and typing settings.
Then go to the personalization and find the colours section. once you've clicked on it you should see the choose your mode setting and set that to dark.
